          Business Intelligence and Data Engineering

          At Amazon, we understand the vital role data plays in our world-class customer service and operations. We believe the best way to achieve this is to employ the most talented minds in the business.

          Sharpening the cutting edge of our business

          Our business intelligence and data engineering professionals track the whole Amazon customer journey, from search through purchase, to dispatch and feedback. This gives us unique insights into our customers’ habits and preferences, so we can continue to improve and innovate on their behalf.

          We work with teams across the company to design the software and platforms that keep us at the cutting edge of everything we do. We spot product trends, find solutions to pain points, and develop tools that smooth our fulfillment processes and boost our data protection. You’ll be joining a group of people whose dials are all set to the future.
